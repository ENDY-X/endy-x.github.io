title: "矩阵相似的性质"
---
矩阵相似的性质总结如下：
{{< katex display=true >}}
\begin{aligned}
& A \sim B \Rightarrow 
    \left\{
        \begin{aligned}
            & A \cong B \Rightarrow r(A) = r(B) \\
            & A,B \text{ 有相同的特征值} \Rightarrow 
                \left\{
                    \begin{aligned}
                        & |A| = |B| \\
                        & \operatorname{tr}(A) = \operatorname{tr}(B)
                    \end{aligned}
                \right. \\
            & A,B \text{ 的可逆性相同} \\
            & A^{-1} \sim B^{-1},\ A^{*} \sim B^{*} \\
            & A^{T} \sim B^{T} \\
            & kA \sim kB \\
            & f(A) \sim f(B) \\
            & A^{m} \sim B^{m}
        \end{aligned}
    \right.
\end{aligned}
{{< /katex >}}
